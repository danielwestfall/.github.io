About Me

As a professional accessibility auditor, I bring a unique background to the table that spans construction quality assurance, customer service, entrepreneurship, and full-stack web development. With a detail-oriented and meticulous approach, I've honed my skills in ensuring high-quality results in every project I undertake.

My diverse experiences include running table games and dealing in annual baccarat tournaments, building and running an award-winning coffee shop from scratch, and developing my skills in full-stack web development and UX design through MIT xPro and Google certificates. I'm well-versed in tools like ANDI, WCAG Color Contrast Checker, Section 508 Trusted Tester, WCAG 2.1, and WAI ARIA, and I'm experienced in manual and automated accessibility testing. I'm committed to making the digital world a more inclusive and accessible place for everyone.
