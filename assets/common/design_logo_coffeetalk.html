Logo Design

I designed this logo for Coffee Talk, a company that my wife and I started together. I tied together the obvious elements of the company name with two whisps of steam that form faces of people talking over a coffee cup. We wanted to create a logo that was simple and easy to read, but also had a sense of warmth and a sense of community.

I designed this using Inkscape, an open source vector graphics editing program.
